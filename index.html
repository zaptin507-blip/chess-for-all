♕

Moves any number of squares in any direction. Most powerful!

♖

♗

♘

Knight

Moves in "L" shape: 2+1 squares. Can jump over pieces!

♙

Pawn

Moves forward 1 square (2 on first move). Captures diagonally.

♙ Pawn Special Rules

👑 Promotion

When a pawn reaches the opposite end of the board (8th rank for White, 1st rank for Black), it MUST be promoted to a Queen, Rook, Bishop, or Knight.

💡 Most players choose Queen (strongest piece), but you can underpromote to a Knight for tactical reasons!

⚡ En Passant (In Passing)

If a pawn moves 2 squares forward from its starting position and lands next to an enemy pawn, that enemy pawn can capture it "in passing" as if it only moved 1 square.

Example: White pawn on e5, Black pawn on d7. Black plays d5 (2 squares). White can capture en passant: exd6 (Black pawn removed, White pawn moves to d6).

⚠️ En passant must be done IMMEDIATELY on the next move, or the right is lost!

🏰 Castling

Castling is the ONLY move where you move two pieces at once (King and Rook). It's used to get your King to safety and activate your Rook.

👈 Kingside (Short)

King moves 2 squares toward the h-file Rook (O-O). Rook jumps over to the other side.

👉 Queenside (Long)

King moves 2 squares toward the a-file Rook (O-O-O). Rook jumps over to the other side.

⚠️ Rules (ALL must be true):

  • Neither the King nor the Rook has moved before
  • No pieces between the King and Rook
  • King is NOT currently in check
  • King does NOT pass through a square under attack
  • King does NOT end up in check

⚔️ Check & Checkmate

⚠️ Check

When a King is under direct attack by an enemy piece.

You MUST get out of check immediately by:

  • Moving the King to safety
  • Capturing the attacking piece
  • Blocking the attack (not possible vs Knights)

👑 Checkmate

When a King is in check and there is NO legal move to escape.

The game is over! The player who delivered checkmate wins.

💡 Key Concepts:

  • Goal: Checkmate your opponent's King
  • You CANNOT make a move that puts your own King in check
  • Stalemate = Draw (King not in check but has no legal moves)
